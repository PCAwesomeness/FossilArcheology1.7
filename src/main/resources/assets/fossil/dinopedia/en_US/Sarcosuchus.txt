Sarcosuchus "Flesh Crocodile"
"SAR-koh-SOO-kuss"

Length: 12m
Height: 1.3m
Area: Africa and South America
Climate: Tropical
Period: Early Cretaceous
Family: Pholidosauridae

Sarcosuchus were ambush 
predators, feeding on 
aquatic and terrestrial animals.
Their eyes are positioned high 
on their head, allowing them to 
see with minimal exposure
of their body.

It is renowned for its large size
and its unusually bulbous nose, 
similar to that of a gharial.
